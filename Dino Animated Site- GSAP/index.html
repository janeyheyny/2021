to Era

Era is home to a whole host of dinosaur attractions including two world class roller coasters and a fantastic educational museum. Lifelike animatronic Dinosaurs can also be found. Jurassic themed family rides, a prehistoric Dinosaur adventure play park and an amazing opportunity to see animatronic Dinosaurs!

Rides

Velocity-raptor

Soar high into the skies on a mighty and unique 395m suspended dinosaur coaster, which takes family adventurers swooping high and low around the prehistoric landscape with speeds over 30mph!

Terror-dactyl

A prehistoric massive roller coaster, perfect for adrenaline junkies who love dinosaurs with a top heigh of 100M above ground.

Edu

Triassic Museum

The Triassic museum tells the story of dinosaurs from all eras. Realistic dioramas of ancient landscapes with fleshed-up reconstructions allow viewers to escape to a different time. Experience fossils brought back to life.

Animatronic Dinosaurs

Our animatronic dinosaurs have a realistic appearance, vivid shape, and lifelike movements. We are one of a few parks in the world to provide our visitors with this experience.
